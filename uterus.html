Uterus


Ấn để chuyển qua tiếng Việt
  • The uterus is an internal organ in females, situated in the lower abdomen, and situated between the vaginal mucosa and the ovaries.
  • It has a width of about 4 - 5 cm and a length of approximately 7 - 10 cm, resembling an inverted pear shape, with a thick wall composed of multiple layers.
  • Its structure includes the uterine fundus, body, cervix, and cervical canal.
  • The uterus assists in regulating blood flow during the menstrual cycle, supports egg implantation and prepares for pregnancy, provides an environment for fertilized egg implantation, and nurtures the developing fetus.
